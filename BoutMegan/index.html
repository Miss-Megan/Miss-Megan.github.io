Career Switch-a-roo

My previous career path was in social science. I studied psychology in undergrad (BA in psych from Syracuse University) and social work in grad school (MSW from Columbia University).

I am very proud of being the first social work intern in the federal prison system for the Southern District of New York, which includes Manhattan and the Bronx (*ding ding* 'Law In Order' chime)... If you've ever seen Law and Order - that's where most of my work took place.

I loved the work I did for Federal Defenders, however, social work is pretty emotionally taxing, and I got burned out. I loved my clients, but bureaucracies wore me down. Maybe one day I'll go back to social work, for now, it's tech world full-speed ahead!
